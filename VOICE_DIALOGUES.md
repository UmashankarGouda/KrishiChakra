# Voice Dialogues for Recording

These are the 6 questions that the AI assistant will ask farmers. Please provide voice recordings in both **Hindi** and **English** for each.

## Format Required
- Female voice (warm, friendly tone)
- Clear pronunciation
- MP3 or WAV format
- File naming: `q1_en.mp3`, `q1_hi.mp3`, etc.

---

## Question 1: Field Name
**English**: "What is your field name?"  
**Hindi**: "आपके खेत का नाम क्या है?"  
**Files needed**: `q1_en.mp3`, `q1_hi.mp3`
